03 Process

Fragments from the edit

F·01

Motion studies

Every transition is choreographed before it's coded. Ease curves are tested against reduced-motion fallbacks. Motion must clarify intent — never perform for its own sake.

F·02

Type rhythm

Balancing serif drama with sans readability for long-form case study storytelling. Whitespace is designed — not defaulted.

F·03

Atmosphere controls

Grain and light leaks tuned as subtle texture, never as blockers. Atmosphere should whisper. It should never shout.

F·04

Interface as choreography

Each interaction must clarify intent. Scroll pacing, reveal timing, hover evolution — all designed as a sequence, not a list of features.

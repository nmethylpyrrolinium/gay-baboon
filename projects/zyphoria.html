Summary

Zyphoria explores the border between a landing page and a cinematic product world. It is designed to feel strange, soft, and lucid.

Concept

The project leans into suspended gradients, atmospheric hierarchy, and small moments of motion that suggest a world beyond the viewport.

Design / Build

Built with lightweight web primitives, restrained animation, and responsive sections that preserve the dreamlike pacing without scroll-jacking or heavy libraries.

Reflection / Outcome

The work became a study in control: letting an interface feel immersive while remaining clear, performant, and usable.
